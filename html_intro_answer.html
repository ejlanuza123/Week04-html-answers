2. Educational Webpage: Interstellar

Overview

Interstellar is a sci-fi epic directed by Christopher Nolan that explores space travel, time relativity, and human survival.

Main Characters

  • Cooper - A NASA pilot on a mission to save humanity.
  • Murph - Cooper's daughter and a brilliant scientist.
  • Brand - A determined scientist and astronaut.

Plot Summary

Earth is dying due to climate change. A secret NASA mission sends Cooper and his team through a wormhole near Saturn to find a habitable planet.

Setting

  • Earth - A dystopian future with food shortages.
  • Space & Other Planets - Includes a water-covered world and an ice planet.

My Thoughts

Interstellar is a breathtaking film that blends science, emotions, and philosophy.
